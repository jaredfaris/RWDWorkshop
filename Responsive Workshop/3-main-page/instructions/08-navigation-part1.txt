Place inside of navigation section of the grid.

            <nav>
                <ul class="nav nav-pills">
                    <li class="active"><a href="index.html">Inventory</a></li>
                    <li><a href="#">Data Entry</a></li>
                    <li><a href="#">Manufacturers</a></li>
                </ul>
            </nav>

Add class of nav to UL to make this a nav bar.

nav-pills or nav-tabs added to the UL styling will make pills or tabs.

nav-justified added to UL styling will make tabs or pills of equal widths.
